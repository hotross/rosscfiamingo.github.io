# we fix the hard stuff.

Dash Labs is a one-person technical services operation out of Brisbane. That one person is me, Ross Fiamingo. 15 years of hands-on experience across mining, agriculture, research and robotics. What sets me apart is the ability to look past the immediate problem and find the underlying issue. Anyone can replace a part. Not everyone can tell you why it failed.
